# IDENTIFY Function

## Purpose

The IDENTIFY function develops organizational understanding to manage cybersecurity risk to systems, assets, data, and capabilities.

## Contents

This directory contains:

### 1. Asset Inventory
- `asset-inventory.md` - Complete inventory of SMB digital assets
- Hardware, software, data repositories, and infrastructure

### 2. Threat Analysis
- `threat-landscape.md` - Common threats facing SMBs
- Ransomware, phishing, insider threats, supply chain risks

### 3. Risk Assessment
- `risk-assessment.md` - Comprehensive risk identification and scoring
- Likelihood and impact analysis for identified risks

### 4. Organizational Context
- `business-context.md` - SMB business profile and critical functions
- Dependencies, stakeholders, and risk tolerance

## Key Deliverables

1. **Asset Mapping** - All critical systems and data identified
2. **Threat Model** - Threats and attack vectors documented
3. **Risk Register** - Risks ranked by severity and impact
4. **Organizational Policies** - Risk management strategies defined

## Next Steps

Once identification is complete, proceed to the PROTECT function (02-Protect/) to implement controls.
